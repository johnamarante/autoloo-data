Autoloo

A Waterloo-Themed Auto Battler by John Amarante

About the Game

Autoloo is a Waterloo-themed auto battler where historical generals clash in fast-paced, strategic skirmishes. Assemble your troops, optimize your formation, and outgun your opponents in a stylized retelling of one of history's most iconic battles.
